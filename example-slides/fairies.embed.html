About Fairies

On fairy safari I have seen a lot of different fairies. They are not equal. They are as individual and colourful as you and me. Even they have different ethnics and therefore different cultures. On the planet I was, there were different fairy folks everywhere. From fairies living in the sky on clouds, wood faries, beach fairies to water faries, you will definitely find the fairy folk you favourize most. Trust me and let me tell you about my findings here.

Sky Faries

Let’s start first with the fairies living in the sky on clouds. I prefer to call them Skyries.

Sky Faries - Skyries

  • Skin colour: white and sky blue

  • Accent colour: white and golden

  • Character: very courteous

  • Culture: protection is deeply rooted in their daily behaviour and their celebrities.

  • Behaviour: Acting like guardians - shielding the planet from danger from the infinite air less universe.

For the duration of stay it was mandatory to wear wings so I could navigate through the sky and clouds. Luckily there are wing stores across the planet.
